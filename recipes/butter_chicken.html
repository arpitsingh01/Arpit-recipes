Butter Chicken

An easy butter chicken recipe I've perfected over the years and shared with many others. The sauce is perfect when creamy and not too thick or too thin. The level of salt and spices can be suited to your taste. Garam and tandoori masala can be found in the ethnic aisle of your grocery store

    Ingredients:

  • ½ cup butter, divided
  • ½ onion, minced and ½ tablespoon minced garlic
  • ½ (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • ¾ pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 1 tablespoon tandoori masala and ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

    Instructions:

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add chicken and coat well. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour (or overnight for more flavor).
  3. Grill, pan-fry, or bake the chicken until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  4. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat.Add onions and sauté until soft and golden (5-7 min).
  5. Stir in garlic, ginger, and spices (curry powder, coriander) and cook for 1 minute then, add tomato purée and sugar; simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. After adding some cream, Simmer for another 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens and chicken is tender.
  7. Taste and adjust salt, sweetness, or cream to keep it mild.Garnish with a little butter or cream swirled on top.
  8. Serve with naan or basmati rice.
