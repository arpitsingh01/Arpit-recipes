Hyderabadi Chicken biryani

If you love biryani, this hyderabadi chicken biryani will WOW you. This layered biryani is so flavorful and will become your go to biryani recipes. You will love this easy and delicious recipe!

    Ingredients:

    For chicken:

  • 500g (1 lb) boneless chicken (or bone-in if you prefer)
  • 2 tbsp oil/ghee
  • ½ cup thick yogurt
  • 2 small onion, finely chopped, 1 tbsp ginger&gsrlic paste
  • 1 tsp paprika (for color, not heat) and ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp ground cumin and 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A few mint leaves and coriander leaves (chopped) and juice of ½ lemon

    • For rice:

  • 1½ cups basmati rice
  • spices: 1 bay leaf, 4 green cardamoms, 4 cloves, 1 small cinnamon stick
  • Salt (for boiling water)

    • For layering:

  • 1 large onion (sliced & fried till golden brown)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • A few saffron strands soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (or yellow food color in milk)
  • Extra mint and coriander leaves

    Instructions:

    Marination and rice:

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients and coat the chicken well.Cover and refrigerate.
  2. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 mins.Boil water with salt and whole spices.
  3. Add rice and cook till 70-80% done (grains should be firm but not raw).Drain and set aside.
  4. Fry sliced onions in oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towel. Set aside.

    5. Dum time:

  5. Fry sliced onions in oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towel. Set aside.
  6. In a thick-bottomed pan (or Dutch oven), spread the marinated chicken in a layer.Top with half of the rice.
  7. Sprinkle some fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
  8. Layer remaining rice and repeat toppings and drizzle ghee/butter on top.
  9. Cover the pan tightly (use dough or foil to seal lid).Cook on low flame for 20-25 mins on gas.
  10. Let it rest 10 mins before opening.
  11. Serve with raita or salad.
